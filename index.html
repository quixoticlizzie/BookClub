About Us

Once upon a time, we were just a bunch of pals who loved books, squeezing into someone's living room for our chats. But we're ran out of space!

 

So, we had this genius idea: why not hit up the local coffee joint where the treats are as good as the reads?

 

Now we're diving into stories with lattes and cakes that'll blow your socks off.

 

Our members come from a wide range of backgrounds - enthusiastic local students seeking new worlds, to seasoned professionals finding solace in the written word, and even the retired souls who share their lifetime of wisdom.
